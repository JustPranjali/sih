 Scholarship Programs in Kerala

        
        
 
 1. Kerala State Higher Education Council (KSHEC) Scholarships


 
KSHEC offers various scholarships and financial assistance programs for students pursuing higher education in Kerala. 
    These scholarships support students at different levels of education, including undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral studies. 
     For more information, visit the KSHEC Scholarships Page.



     

2. Post-Matric Scholarship (PMS) for Minority Students



The Post-Matric Scholarship (PMS) for Minority Students is provided by the Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India.
    It is available to minority students (Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Parsis, and Jains) in Kerala who are studying at the post- matriculation level.
    The scholarship aims to provide financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections.
    For more information, visit the PMS for Minority Students Page


     

3. Kerala State Merit Scholarship (KMS)



The Kerala State Merit Scholarship (KMS) is a program by the Kerala State Council for Science, Technology, and Environment (KSCSTE). 
    It is designed to encourage meritorious students to pursue higher education in science. 
    For more information, visit the KMS Scholarship Page
